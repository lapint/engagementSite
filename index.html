Our Story

HOW WE MET

Lindsey was at a happy hour with Erin, Luke's cousin, in Chicago when he visited for a job interview. He met up with their group and the sparks started flying from day one. It took another year before Luke convinced her to ask him out, but they've never looked back since :)!

Le Proposal

Luke took Lindsey to one of the highest points around Santa Barbara for a Saturday morning hike with friends. At the peak, he asked her to get on a boulder to take a picture together. When they were all set, Luke got down on one knee and popped the question on top the world, and of course, she said yes! It was a race down from the hike to celebrate the best day of Luke's life (and Lindsey's!) with a champagne brunch and close friends.
